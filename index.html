Program Sections


Explore our hockey club's diverse Program Sections, each meticulously crafted to cater to different age groups and skill levels. From Juniors to Professionals, find the perfect program to elevate your game.

Junior Program


The Junior Program at our hockey club provides a dynamic learning environment for young enthusiasts aged 6-12. Focused on skill development, it instills a passion for the sport while fostering teamwork. Coaches employ engaging drills to enhance fundamental skills, laying a solid foundation for players' future growth in hockey.


Teenager Program


In the Teenager Program, players aged 13-17 experience a more advanced training regimen. The emphasis shifts towards refining techniques, tactical awareness, and physical conditioning. This program aims to prepare participants for competitive play, nurturing their potential for higher-level leagues and promoting leadership qualities crucial for team success.


Professional Program


Our Professional Program is tailored for seasoned players aiming to elevate their game. Geared towards those aged 18 and above, it offers intensive training, strategic gameplay analysis, and specialized coaching. Participants benefit from a high-performance environment, gaining insights into the intricacies of professional hockey, enhancing their skills for a potential career in the sport.
